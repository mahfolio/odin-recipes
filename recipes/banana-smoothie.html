Chocolate Peanut Butter Banana Smoothie

Description

A delicious and creamy chocolate peanut butter banana smoothie is sure a great way to start your day. It tastes like a chocolate smoothie even though no chocolate has been added! Itâ€™s a great way to satisfy your sweet tooth with something healthy.

Ingredients

  • 1 banana
  • 1 cup milk 240 ml
  • 2 tablespoons (6 tablespoons) peanut butter
  • 5 small dates or 2 tablespoons maple syrup
  • 1 tablespoon cocoa powder preferably Dutch-processed
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Measure out all the ingredients.
  2. Add all the ingredients into the blender container.
  3. Mix thoroughly (I like to mix it for a good 2-3 minutes) until smooth and creamy (if using dates, make sure to read my notes that are written below).
